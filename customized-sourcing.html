What’s Customized Sourcing ?

Customized Sourcing are free services provided by TAITRA to help buyers find suitable suppliers in an easy and efficient way

  • Submit Request

    Buyer chooses the way they want to find supplier, fill in requests and submit online

  • Suppliers Respond and TAITRA Screens

    TAITRA sourcing specialist shortlist suitable suppliers who response to the request

  • Get Quotes / Meet

    Buyer receive suppliers lists or a meeting schedule
