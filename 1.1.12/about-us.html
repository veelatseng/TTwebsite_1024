About Us

Taiwantrade is an interactive e-business portal designed to provide small and medium enterprises (SMEs) with immediate access to B2B e-commerce activities, and to help them gain a digital edge over their competitors worldwide. Backed by a wealth of trade promotion resources and strong support from different trade and industry associations, e-marketplaces, and popular trade websites, Taiwantrade offers the utmost online trade and promotion services to businesses in Taiwan and abroad.

Major local trade and industry associations representing such sectors as machinery, electronics, communications, computers, auto parts, and motorcycles work closely with Taiwantrade to enable global buyers get hold of all kinds of information on products and manufacturing services offered by Taiwanese enterprises from different industries.

Through latest web technology, Taiwantrade enables buyers around the world and Taiwanese suppliers to conduct RFQ (Request for Quotation) and other business activities online.

Taiwantrade boasts invaluable features which include customized service, efficient and accurate search engine, enterprise-portals, easy-to-use RFQ system, value-added databases, and effective online marketing mechanism. Moreover, its "one-to-one" marketing tool offers customized information, thus saves Taiwantrade members the hassle of wading through a mass of unrelated data.

Expanding Partnerships

Taiwantrade has already been regarded as an integrated trade sourcing service provider. Having achieved great success in trade promotion over the years, it now embarks on expanding its scope of services to include trade facilitation. As part of this effort, it has teamed up with a shipping and logistics provider to facilitate trade transactions. And for its long-term goal, it aims to expand partnership with trade-related service entities and well-established e-marketplaces.
