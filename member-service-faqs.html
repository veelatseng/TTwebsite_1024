How do I join Taiwantrade?

From Taiwantrade's homepage, click on the Join Free link. Follow the step-by-step instructions to quickly complete the registration process. Once you reply our confirm letter sent via email, you may access your very own My Taiwantrade pages

What are the advantages of membership?

Please visit the Membership Services link for details

How do I end my membership?

If you want to take your name off of Taiwantrade's membership list, please email e-member@taiwantrade.com.tw . If possible, let us know the reason why you wish to discontinue membership, as we are concerned about providing the best services that we can. In your email, include your User Name, company name, and the original email address used during registration. Without this complete information, we may not be able to remove you from our system.

Who will see my membership information?

All information submitted to Taiwantrade is held in the strictest of confidence. For details about our sharing policies, please see the Membership Agreement.

User Name & Password

What if I forgot my Password?

imply visit the Taiwantrade homepage, click on the Login link, then select Forgot Password. Fill in the form and Get Password. Taiwantrade's system will immediately send you a new password by email.
You may also contact e-member@taiwantrade.com.tw . Write "Forgot Password" in the Subject line, and include your User Name, company name, and the email address listed during your original registration.

What if I forgot my User Name or original email address?

Contact e-member@taiwantrade.com.tw and write "Forgot User Name" or "Forgot Original Email Address" in the Subject line. Explain your situation and include whatever information that you do recall. To speed up the process, you should try to include any of the following: User Name, company name, email address used during your original registration; also add any other information (i.e. telephone numbers, fax numbers, or company address) to help us identify your company in our database.
